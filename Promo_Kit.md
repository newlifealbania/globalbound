## Quick tips

- Personalize the first sentence of every outreach message. Generic copy-paste gets ignored.
- Lead with value (free account to try) before you ask for anything.
- Target creators whose audience is expats, nomads, movers-abroad, or slow travelers, not just anyone with a big following.
- Give each creator both a link and a unique code, and remind them to disclose the partnership.
