Add Global Bound to your phone

It installs like a real app: an icon on your home screen, full-screen, no app store needed. Takes about 20 seconds.

Open globalbound.app →

Open that link on your phone first, then follow your device below.

📱 iPhone & iPad (Safari)

  1. Open globalbound.app in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button — the square with an arrow pointing up — in the bottom bar.
  3. Scroll down and tap “Add to Home Screen.”
  4. Tap Add in the top corner. Done! 🎉
Tip: it has to be Safari for this to work — not Chrome. If you don't see “Add to Home Screen,” scroll down in the share menu.

🤖 Android (Chrome)

  1. Open globalbound.app in Chrome.
  2. Tap the Install button if it pops up — or tap the ⋮ menu (top right).
  3. Choose “Install app” (or “Add to Home screen”).
  4. Tap Install. Done! 🎉
Tip: the app also shows a little “Install” bar at the bottom — one tap and you're set.

Once it's on your home screen, just tap the globe icon to open Global Bound anytime.
